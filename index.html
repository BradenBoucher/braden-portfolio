Effect of Silver Epoxy Bonding of III-V Laser Dies on Silicon for LiDAR Applications

CLEO 2026 · First Author

Bonding micron-scale III-V laser dies to silicon using silver epoxy. Wall-plug efficiencies up to 20%, infrared output above 10 mW, and a modest efficiency reduction from 14.9% to 14.2% post-bonding.
